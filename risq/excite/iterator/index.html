§Excitation Iterators (excite::iterator)

This module provides convenient iterators for generating excitations based on the pre-computed data in ExciteGenerator. These iterators handle the logic of iterating through determinants, initial orbitals, and target orbitals, applying screening thresholds and validity checks.
