pub fn pt(h_psi: &Wf, e0: f64) -> f64
Expand description

Evaluate the PT expression given H * Psi and E0 Calculates the PT2 energy sum given the first-order interacting wavefunction h_psi.

Computes sum_a |(H*psi)_a|^2 / (E0 - E_a), where a iterates over the determinants in h_psi, (H*psi)_a is det.coeff, E_a is det.diag, and E0 is the reference energy. Assumes det.diag is Some for all determinants in h_psi.
