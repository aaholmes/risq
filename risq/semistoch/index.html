§Semistochastic Methods (semistoch)

This module implements semistochastic approaches, primarily focusing on Epstein-Nesbet perturbation theory (ENPT2) combined with stochastic sampling. The goal is to capture the bulk of the PT2 correction deterministically while efficiently estimating the remaining contributions via sampling.
